Core rights

  • You may contact consumer reporting agencies directly to dispute information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Accurate, current, and verifiable negative information cannot lawfully be removed simply because it is unfavorable.
  • You should receive required disclosures before signing a credit-repair contract.
  • Federal law provides a cancellation period for covered credit-repair contracts.
  • Credit-repair organizations may not make false or misleading statements.
