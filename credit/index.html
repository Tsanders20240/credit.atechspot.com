Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers before you act.

Does this site provide credit repair?

Yes. A+ Credit Education Center may provide educational resources and separately contracted credit-repair services. Services, pricing, cancellation rights, and required disclosures should be provided in writing before work begins.

Can accurate negative information be removed?

Accurate, current, and verifiable information generally cannot be removed simply because it is negative. Disputes should focus on information believed to be inaccurate, incomplete, duplicated, outdated, or unverifiable.

Can you guarantee a score increase?

No. No specific score change, deletion, approval, or financial outcome is guaranteed.

Should I upload sensitive documents here?

No. Do not send Social Security numbers, full account numbers, government IDs, dates of birth, or complete credit reports through an unsecured form.
