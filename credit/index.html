Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you act.

Does this site provide credit repair?

Yes. A+ Credit Restoration provides education, consultations, credit-report review, and factual dispute-document support where legally permitted and only after required disclosures and a written agreement. We do not guarantee a score increase, deletion, financing approval, or a specific timeline.

Can accurate negative information be removed?

We do not claim that accurate, current, and verifiable information can be removed simply because it is unfavorable.

Can I dispute information myself?

Yes. Consumers may dispute inaccurate or incomplete information directly with consumer reporting agencies at no charge.
