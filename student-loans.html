Student Loans: Credit & Resolution Options

Understand how federal or private loan status may affect credit and where to seek official help.

Identify the loan type

Federal and private loans have different servicers, programs, and dispute procedures.

Review federal records

Use StudentAid.gov to review federal loan balances, status, servicers, and available actions.

Check reporting accuracy

Compare payment status, balances, dates, and servicer information against your records.

Address delinquency promptly

Contact the servicer about available repayment, rehabilitation, consolidation, deferment, or forbearance options as applicable.

School misconduct concerns

Borrower defense and closed-school discharge have specific eligibility rules and official applications.

Use qualified help

For lawsuits, garnishment, bankruptcy, or complex discharge issues, consult a qualified attorney or official federal resource.
