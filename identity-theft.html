Identity Theft Recovery Steps

Use official recovery tools, secure your accounts, and organize documentation for fraudulent reporting.

Secure accounts

Change compromised passwords, enable multifactor authentication, and contact affected financial institutions.

Place a credit freeze

Contact Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion directly. A freeze can help block new-account fraud.

Use IdentityTheft.gov

Create an FTC identity-theft report and personalized recovery plan.

Review all three reports

Look for accounts, addresses, inquiries, and collections you do not recognize.

Submit documentation

Provide identity-theft documentation through official bureau and creditor channels.

A consultation can help organize the paperwork, but it does not replace law enforcement or legal counsel.
