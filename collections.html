Collections

Collections: Validation, Reporting & Documentation

Understand the difference between debt validation, credit-report disputes, settlement discussions, and legal defenses.

Identify the collector

Confirm the company, original creditor, amount, dates, and account reference.

Preserve notices

Save collection letters and envelopes. Deadlines can matter.

Request information

Use applicable validation rights and ask for enough information to identify the debt.

Compare credit reports

Check ownership, amount, dates, status, duplicates, and whether the collector is reporting.

Do not make false claims

Only dispute facts you genuinely believe are inaccurate or incomplete.

Seek legal help when needed

Lawsuits, garnishment, judgments, and limitations periods require state-specific legal advice.
