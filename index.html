Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you begin.

Can you guarantee a specific credit-score increase?

No. Credit scores depend on many factors and can change as report information, balances, payment history, and scoring models change.

Can accurate negative information be removed?

We do not claim that accurate, current, and verifiable information can be removed. Support focuses on potentially inaccurate, incomplete, duplicated, outdated, or unverifiable reporting.

Do I need to share my passwords?

No. Never submit online banking passwords, credit bureau passwords, full Social Security numbers, or complete payment-card details through the website intake.

Is this legal or financial advice?

No. Services are educational and administrative in nature. Consult a qualified professional for legal, tax, lending, bankruptcy, or investment advice.

Important consumer notice

A+ Credit Restoration is not a credit bureau, lender, law firm, or government agency. We do not create a new credit identity, misrepresent information, or advise consumers to dispute information they know is accurate.
