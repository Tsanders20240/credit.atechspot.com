Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you act.

Does this site provide credit repair?

No. This site provides education, checklists, calculators, and links to official resources. It does not sell credit-repair services.

Can accurate negative information be removed?

This site does not claim that accurate, current, and verifiable information can be removed.

Should I upload my credit report here?

No. Do not upload credit reports, IDs, Social Security numbers, dates of birth, bank details, or full account numbers.

Can you guarantee a score increase?

No. Scores can change for many reasons, and no specific outcome is guaranteed.

Important educational disclaimer

This website does not provide legal advice, financial advice, lending decisions, or paid credit-repair services. It does not promise a score increase or removal of accurate, current, verifiable information. Do not send Social Security numbers, credit reports, IDs, dates of birth, bank details, or full account numbers.
