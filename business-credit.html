Business Credit Basics

Separate personal and business records while building a documented business profile.

Key areas to review

  • Business entity records
  • EIN and bank account
  • Vendor terms
  • Payment history
  • Personal guarantees

Use accurate documentation

Keep copies of statements, letters, screenshots, notices, and delivery records. Do not alter documents or dispute information you know is accurate.

Educational note: Laws, reporting practices, and account details vary. Use official resources and qualified professionals for advice about your specific situation.
